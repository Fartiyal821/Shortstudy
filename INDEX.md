## 🎯 COMMON QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

### Q: How do I access the admin panel?
**A:** Go to `/g` in your browser. Password is `123`.
See: **ADMIN_GUIDE.md** - Section "Accessing Admin Panel"

### Q: How do I add a course?
**A:** Fill the form in admin panel and click "Add Course to Website".
See: **QUICK_REFERENCE.md** - "Add Course in 5 Steps"

### Q: How do I format the YouTube link?
**A:** Use `https://www.youtube.com/embed/VIDEO_ID` format.
See: **QUICK_REFERENCE.md** - "Common YouTube Issues"

### Q: How do I make code blocks copyable?
**A:** Wrap text in quotes `"like this"` in the description.
See: **ADMIN_GUIDE.md** - "Text with Quotes = Copyable Block"

### Q: Where is my course data stored?
**A:** In browser's localStorage (persists across sessions).
See: **ADMIN_SYSTEM.md** - "Data Management" section

### Q: Can I delete a course?
**A:** Yes, click Delete in the "Manage Courses" section.
See: **ADMIN_GUIDE.md** - "Managing Courses"

### Q: The admin page doesn't work?
**A:** Make sure `.htaccess` is in root. Try `/admin.html` directly.
See: **ADMIN_SYSTEM.md** - "Troubleshooting" section

### Q: How do I change the password?
**A:** Edit `admin.js` line 3 and change from `123` to your password.
See: **ADMIN_SYSTEM.md** - "Production Recommendations"
